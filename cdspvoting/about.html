CDSP Student Organization Information and Voting System

About The System

Hello everyone! In behalf of Team Katagumpay, I'am Marco from BSIT 1-42 of Colegio de San Pedro! As a team leader of this project, we are proudly to introduce to you our madely "CDSP Student Organization Information and Voting System". This voting system can help our co-students to view some basic information on an organization and to vote and also to the election admin can manage the election easily.

As an open-source project developed together with my helpful and hardworking teammates Andrei Leyva and Francis Yabes we are here to help you on how to use the system!

Features:

-For Admin
*Can add by importing or manual adding voters and modify them
*Can Add by importing and delete Candidate
*Can monitor election status
*Can print election data (Voters, Candidates, and Election Results)

-For Voters
*Can view basic organization information
*Can Vote

Whats working? *as of June 13, 2021* :
*Login
*Change Password
*View organizations' pages
*Cast Vote (tested on ITSO, OPS, SSC, JPIA, SHS, JHS, ELEM and TEATRO BATINGAW)
*Add and Modify Student/Voter Manually
*Print Voters
*Print Candidates
*Print Election Tally
*Import Feature
*Monitor Election Results (Tested on SSC, ITSO and Teatro)

Currently Fixing *as of June 13, 2021*:
*Import feature (fixed)
*Print Election Tally (fixed)
*Add/Modify Organization
*Add additional forms for other orgs (fixed)
*Progress bar in tally
*Monitor results tally that auto generate reports
*Two factor Authentication

Notes *for now* :
-For Admin, sending voter's credentials can be done by mail merge.
-For Admin, the election tally can be refreshed manually by pressing "F5" button.
-For Admin, we suggest to use the PC to use the admin panel *it is because of the layout of the page*
-For Admin, the admin credentials is integrated on students table, the only to change password on it is to change it inside the students database.
-For Voters, in case you forgot the password you can message the election admin by clicking on the messenger icon at the right bottom of your screen to request for a new password.
-For Voters, live notifications regarding election updates are still on the works. For now, the election admin will post the printed election results after the election thru Facebook.
-For All, if the system fails to load and shows an error like this in your browser "DNS_PROBE_FINISHED_NXDOMAIN" try to visit the site on other devices or try again later. *blame hostinger!*
